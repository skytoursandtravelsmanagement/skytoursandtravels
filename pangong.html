Pangong Tso

Pangong Tso is literally out of this world! Imagine a lake so blue it looks like a piece of the sky fell onto the mountains. It’s super long and actually changes colors from turquoise to deep blue throughout the day. Since it’s sitting at such a high altitude, the air is super crisp and the vibe is just pure peace. It’s the kind of place where you can just sit for hours, listen to Lana Del Rey, and feel like you’re in a movie. If you’re visiting Leh, you honestly can't skip this—it’s the crown jewel of Ladakh and the sunsets there are absolutely insane. For the full experience, we’ve got some killer packages that include private cab transfers from Leh and all the permits you need. We totally recommend a night stay right by the lake because the starlit sky there is unlike anything you’ve ever seen—it’s like a ceiling of diamonds! You can choose between cozy luxury tents or local homestays if you want that authentic mountain feel. Our packages cover your dinner and breakfast, plus we make sure you have plenty of warm blankets because it gets super chilly at night. It’s the perfect way to disconnect from the world and just vibe with nature.
